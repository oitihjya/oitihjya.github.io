About me

I am a resident of Kolkata, India and am currently staying in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, India for my undergraduate studies. I am a 3rd year student of the Hidayatullah National Law University. I have interned in various law firms and offices across the country. I have participated in two moot court competitions.

I had Political Science as my Major Arts paper and will be choosing Corporate Law and Intellectual Property Rights as my Honours papers. I aspire for a law-firm-job, as I love transactional laws, especially Corporate Law and IPR. I want to use my knowledge of law to provide legal services in the course of business.
