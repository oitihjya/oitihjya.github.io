Volunteer Work

I have worked with the Human Rights Law Network(HRLN), Kolkata and participated at a public hearing of victims of BSF torture, organized by the Banglar Manabadhikar Suraksha Mancha(MASUM). My family has been long associated with the charity works of the Ramakrishna Mission.

Positions of Responsibility

I am currently member of the Moot Court Committee of our University and member of the Internship Coordination Committee of our batch. I was part of the Organising Committee of the flagship event of our University: 8th Justice Hidayatullah National Memorial Moot Court Competition, 2016.

I am an active member of the Debating Society of our University and have helped in conducting several literary events in the University.

Hobbies

I write a blog, during my leisure time. I love reciting poems and have had a professional training of elocution of Bengali poems and plays. I had learnt to play the Keyboard for 5 years. I love debating and have represented the University in 4 National Parliamentary Debates.
